Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about counselling

Choice filling is the process where students list their preferred colleges and courses during admission counselling. The order of preferences determines seat allotment based on rank, availability, and counselling rules.

We analyze your rank, category, budget, and preferences to create a personalized choice order that maximizes your chances of getting the best possible college and branch within your profile parameters.

Our initial analysis is completely free. We provide a detailed report and counselling strategy at no cost. Premium personalized support plans are available for those who need dedicated 1-on-1 guidance.

Once you submit your details, our team typically reviews and prepares your personalized report within 24-48 hours. Urgent requests may be processed faster on priority.

We cover JoSAA, CSAB, UPTAC for Engineering; MCC AIQ and state counselling for Medical; AACCC for AYUSH. We also support various state-level counselling processes across India.

While we cannot guarantee specific seat allotment (as it depends on many factors), our optimized choice filling strategy significantly improves your chances of getting a desirable college and branch within your rank range.
